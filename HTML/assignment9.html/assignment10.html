The Importance of Web Standards

Web standards are essential for ensuring that websites are accessible, functional, and user-friendly across different browsers and devices.

Web standards & best practices help maintain the integrity of the web.

HTML Entities

HTML entities are used to display special characters in HTML. Here are some examples:

  • Greater than: >
  • Less than: <
  • Ampersand: &
  • Quotation mark: "
  • Apostrophe: '
  • Copyright symbol: ©
  • Registered trademark: ®
  • Euro sign: €
  • Non-breaking space:  
  • Cent sign: ¢
  • Pound sterling: £
  • Yen sign: ¥
  • Section symbol: §
  • Paragraph symbol: ¶
  • Plus-minus sign: ±
  • Multiplication sign: ×
  • Division sign: ÷
  • Superscript two: ²
  • Superscript three: ³
  • Trademark symbol: ™
  • Left arrow: ←
  • Right arrow: →
  • Up arrow: ↑
  • Down arrow: ↓
  • Omega: Ω
  • Pi: Π
  • Mu: μ
  • Delta: Δ
